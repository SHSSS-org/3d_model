Instructions

  • Google Drive: Share your model file, copy the link, and paste it above
  • Desktop: Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, right-drag to pan
  • Mobile: One finger to rotate, two fingers pinch to zoom, two fingers drag to pan
  • Toggle the torch light to illuminate the model from the camera's perspective
  • Apply custom textures to OBJ models using the texture upload option
